About the Dataset :

This data was collected from direct marketing campaigns of a Portuguese banking institution. The campaigns were carried out by phone calls with the intention of selling Bank Term Deposits. Often more than one phone call was required to be made to the same customer with the intention of closing a certain banking deal.

This is a binary classification dataset with the target variable being whether the customer agrees to close a deal after the campaign or not. Hence, the classification goal is to predict if the customer chooses to subscribe to a term deposit offered by the bank.
